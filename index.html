Our Purpose

We are a community of readers dedicated to exploring ideas through literature. Our discussions challenge perspectives, deepen understanding, and foster meaningful connections.

Whether you're a lifelong reader or just beginning your journey, you'll find a welcoming space for thoughtful dialogue and intellectual growth.

Membership Guidelines

To maintain a respectful and engaging environment, we ask all members to:

  • Come prepared: Read the assigned material before each meeting
  • Respect others: Listen actively and engage thoughtfully with different viewpoints
  • Participate meaningfully: Share your insights while allowing space for others
  • Commit to attendance: Regular participation helps build community
